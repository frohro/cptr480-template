### Part 1 — Extension Project Proposal

Write your proposal in `assignment7/extension_proposal.md`. It must include:

**1. Chosen direction** (one sentence)  
*Example: "We will implement 24-bit/96 kHz UAC2 audio using 32-bit containers and reconfigure the Si5351a and PIO I2S driver for 96 kHz LRCK."*

**2. Motivation** (2–3 sentences)  
Why did you choose this? What does it add to the SDR, the board, or your skills?

**3. Technical approach** (the main section — at least one paragraph per major component)  
For each significant piece of the extension, describe:
- What you will change or add
- What existing code or hardware it builds on
- What new firmware, driver, or configuration is required

**4. Hardware selection**  
Will you stay on the YD-RP2040, or check out a Pico W / Pico 2 / Pico 2W? Justify the choice. If staying on YD-RP2040, note why the RP2040's capabilities are sufficient.

**5. Known risks** (at least 2, specific)  
What could go wrong? What is your contingency if it does?

**6. Minimum viable demo**  
If everything goes wrong and you only get the simplest version working, what will you show at the final presentation?

**7. Stretch goal**  
If everything goes right, what's the most impressive thing this extension could do?
